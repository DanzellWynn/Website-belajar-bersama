English - Present Continuous Tense

📚 Theory

Present Continuous Tense is used for actions happening at the moment of speaking.

  • Structure: Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing.
  • Example: She is studying now.
  • Signal words: now, at the moment, currently.

🧠 Exercises

Question 1: Complete: "They _____ (play) football now."

Answer: are playing.

Question 2: Make a sentence using Present Continuous!

Answer: I am learning English right now.
