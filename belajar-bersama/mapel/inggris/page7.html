English - Passive Voice

📚 Theory

Passive Voice is used when the focus is on the action, not who or what is performing the action.

  • Structure: Subject + to be + past participle (V3).
  • Example: The letter is written by her.
  • Used when the actor is unknown or unimportant.

🧠 Exercises

Question 1: Change to passive: "She makes a cake."

Answer: A cake is made by her.

Question 2: Passive form of "They clean the room"?

Answer: The room is cleaned by them.
