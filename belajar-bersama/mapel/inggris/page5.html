English - Future Tense

📚 Theory

Future Tense is used to express actions that will happen in the future.

  • Structure: Subject + will + Verb1.
  • Example: She will visit her friend tomorrow.
  • Signal words: tomorrow, next week, in the future.

🧠 Exercises

Question 1: Complete: "I _____ (go) to Bali next month."

Answer: will go.

Question 2: Write a sentence using Future Tense!

Answer: They will attend the meeting tomorrow.
