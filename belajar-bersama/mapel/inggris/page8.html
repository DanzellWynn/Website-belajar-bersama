Conditional Sentences express situations and their possible results. There are four types: zero, first, second, third.

  • Zero: facts (If + present, present).
  • First: real possibilities (If + present, will + V1).
  • Second: unreal present (If + past, would + V1).
  • Third: unreal past (If + had + V3, would have + V3).
