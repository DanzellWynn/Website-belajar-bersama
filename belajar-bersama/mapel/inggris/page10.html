English - Question Tags

📚 Theory

Question tags are short questions added at the end of statements to confirm information.

  • Positive statement → negative tag: "You are happy, aren’t you?"
  • Negative statement → positive tag: "She isn’t busy, is she?"
  • Used to seek agreement or clarification.

🧠 Exercises

Question 1: Complete: "It’s cold, _____?"

Answer: isn’t it?

Question 2: Complete: "She can drive, _____?"

Answer: can’t she?
