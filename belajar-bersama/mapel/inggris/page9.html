Reported speech is used to report what someone has said without quoting the exact words.

  • Direct: He said, "I am happy."
  • Reported: He said that he was happy.
  • Change of tense is needed depending on reporting verb.

Question 1: Change to reported: "She said, 'I like coffee.'"

Answer: She said that she liked coffee.

Question 2: Report: "They said, 'We are going to the park.'"

Answer: They said that they were going to the park.
