English - Simple Past Tense

📚 Theory

Simple Past Tense is used to describe actions that happened in the past.

  • Structure: Subject + Verb2.
  • Example: She visited the museum yesterday.
  • Signal words: yesterday, last night, two days ago.

🧠 Exercises

Question 1: Complete: "I _____ (buy) a new book yesterday."

Answer: bought.

Question 2: Write one sentence using Simple Past Tense!

Answer: I watched a movie last night.
